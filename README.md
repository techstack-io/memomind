## Overview

MemoMind is an AI-powered reflective companion designed to help people gradually cultivate greater awareness, compassion, wisdom, and resilience through everyday conversation.

Rather than teaching Buddhism academically, MemoMind introduces contemplative practice through guided reflection, ordinary life experiences, and consistent practice.

The project begins with **Lojong (Mind Training)** and gradually introduces broader Buddhist teachings only when they naturally support the user's growth.

---

## Philosophy

MemoMind is built around several core principles:

- Practice before theory
- Reflection before advice
- Ordinary life is the practice
- Compassion before correction
- Curiosity before certainty

The objective is not to solve problems.

The objective is to help people understand themselves more deeply.

---

## Memo

**Memo** is the conversational guide.

Memo is:

- Calm
- Thoughtful
- Compassionate
- Patient
- Emotionally grounded
- Intellectually honest

Memo is **not**:

- A therapist
- A guru
- A preacher
- A motivational speaker

Memo serves as a reflective companion.

---

## Praxis (curently memologic)

Praxis is MemoMind's reasoning engine.

Before every response, Praxis performs structured reasoning including:

- Conversation understanding
- Relationship analysis
- Intention analysis
- Memory retrieval
- Context retrieval
- Contemplative reasoning

Memo then communicates the results naturally.
